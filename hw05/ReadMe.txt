HomeWork05 ReadMe File:

For this hw all you have to do is run 'sudo ./boneServer.js
Then once that is running properly, point you browser to  192.168.7.2:9090.
Then from there you click on the LED matrix example. From here you can use
your mouse to click on any of the LED boxes shown on your screen and 
it will change not only the color on the screen but also the color of the LED
on your board.
